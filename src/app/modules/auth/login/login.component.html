Features and Purpose of Education Management System
Enquiries and Admissions Management
With the help of the Education Management System, the most commonly asked questions and enquiries regarding admissions, rules and policies can be easily answered. This can free up a lot of time and resources which can be allocated elsewhere.
Management of Fee Collections
Fee collection can be made easy with the help of the Educational Management System, as it calculates and displays fee amount, due and payment details.
Examination Management
An Education Management System can be useful for planning, scheduling, and informing important information about exams by using scheduling. It can also be used to publish exam results that can be accessed by the students and teachers. Further detailed reports can be created using this data, and based on this, student performance can be tracked.

Student Information Management
This feature helps teachers and parents to access essential information on students' academic activities quickly and easily. This may include fee attendance records, exam results, payment history, and general academic progress.

Attendance Management
Attendance tracking and its management is made entirely easier. Teachers and school administrators do not need to track attendance manually but simply evaluate automatically generated reports and check where there are issues.

Lesson Plans & Assignments
An Education Management System makes it easy for teachers to share assignments and lesson plans with students and their parents in advance. These can be downloaded or printed if required.

Automatic Certificate Generation
Using Education Management System, Students' education related certificate and other sports activities can be generated and print easily.

Timetable management
This function allows you to quickly organize and sort out the subjects and time allocated to each of them according to the class and teacher. A hurriedly made timetable will only bring confusion and more hurdles in running your educational institute smoothly.

Evaluation and Assessment This module enables to conduct online test and exams. Evaluation or assessment of the students can be conducted by defining question and answer that can be re-used over and over again that can saves teachers' huge amount of time.
